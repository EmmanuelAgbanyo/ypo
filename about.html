About Us

Igniting apt youth-led initiatives for development

"At Youth Path Organization, we believe in the power of young people to create lasting change. Through innovation, collaboration, and dedication, we uplift communities and inspire a brighter future."

Cyril France, Founder

We are committed to driving youth-led development through initiatives that promote sustainability, education, and social impact. Our mission is to ensure that young people and vulnerable communities have the resources, knowledge, and opportunities to thrive.

What We Do

Learn More What We Do And Get Involved - Our Focus

Regenerative agriculture & organic farming

We engage youth and farming communities in unique programs that help them put into practice regenerative agriculture and sustainable land-use practices to promote biodiversity and a sustainable future.

Learn More

Climate change & environmental sustainability

We liaise with allied organizations within the climate and environmental systems to create space for youth to play an active role as change agents.

Learn More

Peacebuilding & human rights

We advocate, support and promote a society where people are safe from harm, have access to justice, and know their rights and responsibilities. We are building a community of young leaders committed to peacebuilding and the protection of human rights.

Learn More

Policy and governance

We are building a generation of youth who are well-informed and are influencing policies and development processes that shape their lives and that of their communities. We work with youth and allied organizations to create space for youth inclusion in governance at the local, regional and national levels

Learn More

Health and sanitation

Working with young people through a variety of initiatives, including health activism, community outreach and community mobilization we increase access to health and social services for vulnerable populations and low-income households
